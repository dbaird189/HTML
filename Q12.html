Hydrogen is combustible. When we burn hydrogen, the hydrogen combines with oxygen in the air to produce steam, which when condensed, becomes water. The chemical formula is:

2H2 + O2 → 2H2O

To do a chemical formula in a web page, we do not need very complicated text formatting. The main thing is the subscript. But we also need to look up a special character for the right pointing arrow. The HTML mnemonic for right pointing arrow is rarr. Then we put the ampersand "&" before the mnemonic, and a semi-colon ";" after the mnemonic to generate the special character. That is,

&rarr; generates the right arrow "→"
